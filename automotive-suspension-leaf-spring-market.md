<p><p>The automotive suspension leaf spring market is primarily categorized into mono leaf springs and multi leaf springs. Mono leaf springs consist of a single, large leaf, offering a lightweight and compact design ideal for applications requiring reduced weight and space. In contrast, multi leaf springs comprise several stacked leaves, providing enhanced strength and flexibility, which is crucial for heavier vehicles or off-road applications. Both types play essential roles in vehicle stability, load distribution, and ride comfort.</p></p>
