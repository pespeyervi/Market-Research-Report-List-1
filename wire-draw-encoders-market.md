<p><ul><li>Absolute Draw Wire Encoder</li><li>Incremental Draw Wire Encoder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wire draw encoders are critical for position measurement in various applications, offering two primary types: absolute and incremental. Absolute draw wire encoders provide a unique positional value with each measurement, ensuring that the exact position is known even after power loss. In contrast, incremental draw wire encoders measure changes in position from a defined starting point, making them suitable for applications where relative movement is key. Both types serve essential roles in automation and precision engineering.</p></p>
