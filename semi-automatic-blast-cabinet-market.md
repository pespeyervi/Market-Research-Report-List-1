<p><ul><li>Pressure Blast Cabinet</li><li>Suction Blast Cabinet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semi-automatic blast cabinet market primarily involves two types: pressure blast cabinets and suction blast cabinets. Pressure blast cabinets utilize high-pressure systems to propel abrasive materials onto surfaces, offering faster and more efficient cleaning or finishing. In contrast, suction blast cabinets rely on a vacuum mechanism to draw abrasives, providing greater control and reduced material consumption. Both cabinet types cater to diverse industries, including automotive and aerospace, by enhancing surface preparation and restoring equipment with precision and consistency.</p></p>
