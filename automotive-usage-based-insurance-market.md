<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Automotive Usage-based Insurance Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pay-As-You-Drive(PAYD)</li><li>Pay-How-You-Drive(PHYD)</li><li>Manage-How-You-Drive(MHYD)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive usage-based insurance (UBI) consists of various market types that assess driver behavior and vehicle usage to determine premiums. Pay-As-You-Drive (PAYD) charges based on mileage, rewarding low-mileage drivers with lower premiums. Pay-How-You-Drive (PHYD) evaluates driving habits like speed and braking, incentivizing safe driving behaviors. Manage-How-You-Drive (MHYD) goes further by offering feedback and coaching to drivers, encouraging improved driving practices for both safety and cost savings. Each type aims to tailor insurance to individual driving patterns.</p></p>
