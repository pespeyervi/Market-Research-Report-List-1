Pipeline Integrity Management ensures safe operation of pipeline systems, while Hazard Identification (HAZID) studies assess potential risks. Structural Integrity Management evaluates structural safety, and Nondestructive Testing (NDT) Inspection detects flaws without damaging assets. Other services encompass additional asset integrity assessments.</p></p>
