<p><strong>The MEMS Pressure Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capacitive Pressure Sensors</li><li>Piezoresistive Pressure Sensors</li><li>Piezoelectric Pressure Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MEMS pressure sensor market is primarily classified into three types: capacitive, piezoresistive, and piezoelectric pressure sensors. Capacitive pressure sensors detect pressure changes by measuring variations in capacitance, making them highly sensitive and ideal for low-pressure applications. Piezoresistive sensors utilize the change in resistance of semiconductor materials under pressure, offering wide range and robustness. Piezoelectric sensors generate an electrical charge in response to mechanical stress, suitable for dynamic pressure measurements. These technologies cater to diverse applications across automotive, industrial, and medical sectors.</p></p>
