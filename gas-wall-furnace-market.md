<p><p>The Gas Wall Furnace market comprises two main types: noncondensing and condensing. Noncondensing units operate by venting exhaust gases directly outside, offering simpler installation and lower costs but lower efficiency. In contrast, condensing units capture and utilize additional heat from the exhaust through advanced technology, resulting in higher efficiency and lower emissions. While condensing types often come with a higher upfront cost, their long-term energy savings and environmental benefits make them increasingly popular in residential heating solutions.</p></p>
