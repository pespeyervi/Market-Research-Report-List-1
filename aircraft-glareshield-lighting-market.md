<p><p>Aircraft glareshield lighting comprises three main types: incandescent lights, halogen lights, and light-emitting diodes (LED). Incandescent lights offer a warm glow but have lower energy efficiency and shorter lifespans. Halogen lights provide brighter illumination and improved efficiency but generate more heat. LEDs have gained popularity due to their high energy efficiency, long lifespan, and reduced heat output, making them ideal for modern aircraft designs. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing cockpit visibility and pilot comfort.</p></p>
