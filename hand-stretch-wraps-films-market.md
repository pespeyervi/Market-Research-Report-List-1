<p><p>The hand stretch wrap films market is divided into two primary types: machine stretch film and hand stretch wrap. Machine stretch film is designed for automatic wrapping machines, offering high efficiency and consistent tension for large-scale packaging operations. In contrast, hand stretch wrap is manually applied, ideal for smaller loads or less frequent use, providing flexibility and ease of handling. Both types play crucial roles in securing products for transport while optimizing packaging efficiency and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
