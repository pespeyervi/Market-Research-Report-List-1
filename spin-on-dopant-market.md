<p><p>The Spin-on Dopant (SOD) market consists of P-Type and N-Type segments, crucial for semiconductor fabrication. P-Type dopants introduce positive charge carriers, enhancing conductivity by creating "holes" in the material's structure. N-Type dopants, on the other hand, add extra electrons, resulting in enhanced electron mobility. These dopants are essential in tailoring semiconductor properties for various applications, such as integrated circuits and optoelectronics. The demand in both segments is driven by advancements in electronics and the push for miniaturization in devices.</p></p>
