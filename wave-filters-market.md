<p><p>The wave filters market is divided into two main types: active filters and passive filters. Active filters utilize amplifying components, such as operational amplifiers, to enhance signal processing capabilities, offering better performance and flexibility. They are commonly used in applications requiring precise frequency response. In contrast, passive filters are constructed using passive components like resistors, capacitors, and inductors, typically providing lower signal distortion and simpler designs. Each type caters to different needs in electronics, telecommunications, and audio processing applications.</p></p>
