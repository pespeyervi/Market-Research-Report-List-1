<p><p>The plastic pail market primarily consists of two types: High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE) and Polypropylene (PP). HDPE pails are known for their durability, impact resistance, and ability to withstand temperature fluctuations, making them ideal for industrial and food applications. On the other hand, PP pails offer excellent chemical resistance, are lighter, and provide good clarity, making them suitable for consumer goods and packaging. Both materials are prized for their recyclability and versatility across various sectors, including food, pharmaceuticals, and chemicals.</p></p>
