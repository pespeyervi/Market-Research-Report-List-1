<p><p>The thrombus extraction catheter market comprises two primary types: manual thrombus aspiration catheters and mechanical thrombus aspiration catheters. Manual catheters rely on the clinician's skill to manually suction and remove thrombus, allowing for flexible use in various vascular scenarios. In contrast, mechanical thrombus aspiration catheters utilize specialized devices to create suction, providing a more standardized and often more efficient method for thrombus removal. Both types are essential in managing thromboembolic conditions and improving patient outcomes in cardiovascular interventions.</p></p>
