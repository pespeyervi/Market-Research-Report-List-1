<p><p>The radiant conditioning system market is primarily divided into two types: water heating and electric heating. Water heating systems utilize heated water circulated through pipes embedded in floors or walls, providing a uniform warmth and energy efficiency. In contrast, electric heating systems use electric cables or mats installed under flooring to generate heat. Both types offer distinct advantages in comfort and efficiency, catering to varying consumer preferences and building requirements, and are increasingly popular in residential and commercial applications.</p></p>
